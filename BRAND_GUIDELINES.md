# Brand Guidelines

## Complete Brand System

1. **Logo**: Guidelines on logo usage, sizing, and placement.
2. **Color Palette**: Primary, secondary, and accent colors.
3. **Typography**: Fonts for headings, body text, and any special type usage.
4. **Imagery**: Style and usage of photos and illustrations.
5. **Voice and Tone**: Guidelines for content creation and brand messaging.

## Design Tokens

### Colors
- Primary Color: #FF5733
- Secondary Color: #C70039
- Accent Color: #900C3F

### Typography
- Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif
- Body: 'Open Sans', sans-serif

### Spacing
- Small: 4px
- Medium: 8px
- Large: 16px

### Breakpoints
- Mobile: 480px
- Tablet: 768px
- Desktop: 1024px

## Conclusion

These guidelines are essential for maintaining a consistent brand identity across all platforms and touchpoints.
